## About

This repository hosts the published **HalalAI** video files — Quranic recitation
with on-screen translations (Arabic · English · Urdu · German), one release per
surah. The videos are served from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/ibrardu/halalai-videos/releases)
and play in the [HalalAI gallery](https://ibrardu.github.io/quran-video-project/).

The goal is not just beautiful recitation, but **understanding**: every video
pairs the recitation with a careful translation, verse by verse.
